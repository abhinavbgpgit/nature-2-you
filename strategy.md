Natural Farming Website Strategy (React + Tailwind)
Website Ka Main Purpose
Ye website sirf ek normal farming website nahi hogi. Iska main goal hoga:

Logon ko Natural Farming ke bare me educate karna.

Farmers ko inspire karna.

Investors ko business model samjhana.

Bharat aur Bihar me chal rahe Natural Farming movement ko support karna.

Videos, blogs aur visual content ke through trust build karna.

Ek long-term ecosystem banana.

Website Ka Vision
"Prakriti ke saath farming, sehatmand bhojan, sehatmand prithvi aur sustainable income."

Ye website emotional + educational + business tino angle ko cover karegi.

Target Audience
1. Farmers
Chemical farming se pareshan kisan

Kam kharch me farming karna chahte hain

Mitti ki health improve karna chahte hain

2. Investors
Sustainable business me invest karna chahte hain

Long-term agriculture model dekhna chahte hain

Export aur organic market samajhna chahte hain

3. Students & Youth
Farming startup me interest

Environment aur climate change ko lekar aware

4. Common Public
Healthy food chahte hain

Chemical-free lifestyle

Complete UI/UX Planning Strategy
Ab sabse important cheez hai UI/UX planning.

Agar content powerful ho aur UI weak ho to website cheap lagti hai. Natural farming website ko premium documentary + modern startup + emotional ecosystem jaisa feel dena hoga.

Goal: User website open karte hi feel kare:

"Ye koi normal farming website nahi hai. Ye future movement hai."
